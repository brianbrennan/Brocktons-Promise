Promise Partners

If you serve youth and families in Brockton, you’re already helping to make the Promise.
Please link into the coalition by contacting us here.

Why connect with Brockton’s Promise? It seeks to unify all youth- and family-serving initiatives in the community. This allows us to measure the impact that our collective efforts make. Brockton’s Promise also facilitates communication among youth- and family-serving groups across the city, encourages collaboration, and supports evaluation.
